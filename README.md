# E-Commerce Business Performance Analysis using Python

## Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce company’s sales, profit, and customer data using Python. The objective is to evaluate business performance, identify key revenue drivers, and uncover areas for improvement.

The analysis transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights that can support strategic decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales and profit trends  
- Identify high-performing and low-performing product categories  
- Analyze the impact of discounts on profitability  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Segment customers based on revenue contribution and order frequency  
- Analyze regional and city-level performance  

---

## Tools and Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

---

## Analysis Performed
- Monthly sales and profit trend analysis  
- Category and sub-category performance evaluation  
- Customer segmentation and identification of high-value customers  
- Sales-to-profit ratio analysis  
- Shipping mode usage analysis  
- Regional and city-level performance analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales and profit show a clear seasonal trend, with higher performance in later months  
- The technology category contributes the highest share of revenue and profit  
- Some sub-categories generate strong profits, while others consistently incur losses  
- The consumer segment drives the highest sales volume but has relatively lower profit efficiency  
- Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of cities and customers  

---

## Recommendations
- Strengthen inventory planning and marketing strategies during peak demand periods  
- Focus on high-margin product categories to improve profitability  
- Re-evaluate pricing and discount strategies for low-margin segments  
- Improve customer retention through targeted engagement strategies  
- Expand into underperforming regions to diversify revenue sources
